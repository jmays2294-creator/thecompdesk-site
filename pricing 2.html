Common questions
Is the free plan really free forever? ▾
Yes. The calculators, Learn articles, and Find Attorney directory will always be free. We don't put time limits on free accounts.
What happens after the 14-day trial? ▾
Your card isn't charged until the trial ends. If you cancel before then, you won't be billed. After the trial, Pro renews at $19/month until canceled.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Yes — cancel any time from your account page. You'll keep Pro access through the end of the billing period, then drop back to Free.
Do I need a credit card to sign up? ▾
No card required for the Free plan. You only need a card when you start the Pro trial.
Who is The Comp Desk for? ▾
New York State workers' compensation claimants and their attorneys. All calculators are built around NYS WCB rules and rates.
