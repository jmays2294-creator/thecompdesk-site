What to Bring

Before You Arrive

  • • Arrive 10-15 minutes early to complete check-in paperwork
  • • Bring all required documents (see checklist above)
  • • Wear comfortable, clean clothing
  • • Avoid heavy perfume or cologne

During the Exam

  • • The doctor will perform a physical examination
  • • You may be asked to perform movements or exercises
  • • The exam typically lasts 30-60 minutes
  • • You can take notes during or after the exam

Important Reminders

  • • Be honest about your condition and symptoms
  • • The IME doctor works for the insurance company, not you
  • • Do not exaggerate or minimize your injuries
  • • Ask questions if you don't understand something
  • • Bring a friend or family member for support if allowed
