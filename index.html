Built for the hearing room. Growing into your companion.

The Comp Desk started where the need was most obvious — in the hearing room. AWW calculations done on scrap paper, SLU percentages pulled from memory, fee application equations scribbled by hand. We built the calculator that the room was missing, and attorneys started using it every day.

But a calculator was never the finish line. When you're injured at work, the confusion doesn't start at the hearing — it starts the moment you get hurt. What do I file? Where do I go for treatment? What's an IME, and how do I prepare? What am I actually entitled to? The system is complicated, and nobody hands you a guidebook.

That's why The Comp Desk is growing into something bigger. We call the injured worker experience Comp Buddy — a free companion that walks with you from the moment of injury through treatment, hearings, and settlement. We're building a Find a Doctor tool to connect you with authorized providers, IME reminders so you never miss an appointment, a learning portal that explains your rights in plain language, and a direct connection to experienced workers' compensation attorneys who can fight for you.

The calculators are just the beginning. The mission is making sure every injured worker in New York State has the information, the tools, and the support they deserve — without paying a dime.

Know your comp. Fight for your rights.
