Frequently asked questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Both the Pro and Firm tiers include a 7-day free trial. You can cancel anytime during the trial and won't be charged. After the trial, you'll be billed monthly at the plan rate.

What if I only need the SLU calculator?

The Pro tier is your best bet. At $9.99/month, you get the SLU Permanency Checker along with every other advanced calculator. There's no a-la-carte pricing — we keep it simple.

How many attorneys can use the Firm tier?

The Firm tier supports unlimited attorneys within a single practice. Each attorney gets their own login, and case data can be shared across the firm. Contact us if you need specific configuration for your team.

Does this work with Litify / Salesforce?

The Firm tier includes Salesforce/Litify integration for case data syncing and export. We're actively building deeper integrations — reach out to learn what's available for your specific setup.

Can I use this on my phone at hearings?

Absolutely. The Comp Desk is available on iOS (App Store) and as a responsive web app. Pull it up on your phone between hearings, on your tablet at your desk, or on your laptop in the office.

Who built this?

The Comp Desk was built by a practicing NYS Workers' Compensation attorney who got tired of doing math on scrap paper at hearings. Every calculator is grounded in how the Board actually operates — the 2018 Permanency Guidelines, the 2012 Impairment Guide, and 40+ years of statutory rates.

How do I cancel?

Cancel anytime from your account settings — no phone calls, no hoops. Your access continues through the end of your current billing period.
