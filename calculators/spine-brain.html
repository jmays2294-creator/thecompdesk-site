Spinal Cord and Brain Injuries in Workers' Compensation

Spinal cord injuries and traumatic brain injuries are among the most serious workers' compensation cases. These injuries typically result in permanent total disability (PTD), which provides lifetime weekly benefits equal to the worker's compensation rate. The calculation and adjudication of such cases require sophisticated analysis of medical evidence, functional capacity evaluations, and vocational rehabilitation assessments.

The Spine & Brain Calculator helps case professionals accurately assess the full scope of benefits and costs associated with these catastrophic injuries, including present value calculations for lifetime benefits, future medical costs, and rehabilitation services. This information is critical for settlement negotiations, fund distribution, and ensuring adequate compensation for these severe cases.
